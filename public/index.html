Information for Ultimate Tic-Tac-Toe

Welcome to Ultimate Tic-Tac-Toe! This is a strategic and challenging game that takes the classic Tic-Tac-Toe to a whole new level. Here's some detailed information of the game to help you get started:

  1. Objective:
    • The goal of Ultimate Tic-Tac-Toe is to win the overall game by getting three marks in a row (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally) on the large 3x3 board, just like in regular Tic-Tac-Toe.
  2. Game Structure:
    • The game consists of nine smaller 3x3 Tic-Tac-Toe boards, which are placed within the larger 3x3 board. These smaller boards are referred to as "mini-boards."
    • Each mini-board has nine cells, and the large board contains nine mini-boards.
    • Players take turns placing their marks ('X' or 'O') in the cells of the mini-boards.
  3. Rules:
    • The game starts with 'X' as the first player.
    • Players take turns by clicking on an empty cell within the mini-board. The cell they choose determines the next mini-board their opponent must play in.
  4. Background Colors:
    • A red background indicates the next mini-board the player must play in.
    • A yellow background (applicable for Single Player with AI mode only) indicates the last move made in the game.
    • A mini-board with an empty background indicates that any cell is available for play.
  5. Strategy:
    • To win the game, you need to think strategically, as your move determines not only your immediate mini-board but also your opponent's future mini-board.
    • Pay attention to the winning conditions in both the mini-boards and the overall game. Try to block your opponent from winning mini-boards while working towards your victory.
  6. Restarting the Game:
    • You can restart the game at any time by clicking the "Restart Game" button.
  7. Keyboard Shortcuts:
    • Press 'R' to restart the game.
    • Press 'D' to toggle dark-mode.
    • Press 'M' to toggle mute-button.
    • Press 'F' to toggle full-screen mode.
    • Press 'Esc' to close this information-popup
  8. Online Multiplayer Game Mode:
    • Player 1 is assigned to whoever enters the Online Multiplayer Game Mode first.
    • Player 1 and 2 MUST be on the same WI-FI Network.
    • To play the Online Multiplayer Game Mode on the same device:
      1. The host (who’s running this program), who will be Player 1, should select “Online Multiplayer” from the dropdown menu.
      2. The host, who will also be Player 2, should open another tab (in a separate window for convenience) and navigate to “http://localhost:3000”, and select “Online Multiplayer” from the dropdown menu.
      3. Player 1 can start by making a move.
    • To play the Online Multiplayer Game Mode on different devices:
      1. The host (who’s running this program), who will be Player 1, should select “Online Multiplayer” from the dropdown menu.
      2. Player 2 will need to use Player 1's IPv4 address (as they are the host) followed by :3000 (which is the port number) to connect. For example, if Player 1's IPv4 address is 192.168.1.5, Player 2 should enter 192.168.1.5:3000 in their web browser. IPv4 address can be found in “Network and Internet” settings under “Properties”.
      3. The game should now open in Player 2’s browser, and they can select “Online Multiplayer” from the dropdown menu.
      4. Player 1 (the host) can start by making a move.

Now that you know the information and rules, enjoy playing Ultimate Tic-Tac-Toe, and may the best strategist win! Good luck!
